ABOUT US

CAFE + GALLERY = CAFELLERY One will get an artistic vibe while enjoying Cafellery's delicious food. Unique food items are available to make the taste buds dance. Ambience is comprised of canvases, wall paintings, potraits and graffiti. All of which is totally done by the owner itself.
